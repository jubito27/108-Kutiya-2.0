Parichay — Who are they?

Pujya Swami Karun Dass Ji Maharaj ek prasidh Sant hain jo 108 Kutiya, Barsana (Mathura, Uttar Pradesh) mein niwas karte hain. Wo Radha Krishna Parivar Seva Trust (registered) ke sansthapak hain jo Govardhan, Mathura mein sthit hai.

Unka pura jeevan bhakti, sadhana aur sant-sewa mein beeta hai. Unka naam Nimbark Sampraday ke prasidh kathakaron mein aata hai — unke Bhagwat Katha, Ram Katha, Bhaktmal Katha, Raas Leela aur Nikunj Ashtayam Katha ke pravachan poore Bharat mein prasiddh hain.

"Pujya Shri Maharaj Ji ne apne jeevan mein gyarah Santons ka sang kiya aur aaj bhi un sabhi ko apne Guru roop mein sweekar karte hain."

— YouTube Channel Description, Swami Karun Dass Ji

Maharaj Ji ke pravachan Nimbark TV par live aate hain, jinhe lakho shradhalu door-door se sunte hain. Unka YouTube channel 4 lakh se adhik subscribers tak pahuncha hai aur karodon views hain.

✦   108 Kutiya Mein Unka Role

🏛️

Sansthapak — Founder

Swami Ji ne 108 Kutiya, Barsana ki sthapna ki — yeh ek pavitra ashram hai jo Radha-Krishna ki bhakti ka kendra hai. Yahan niyamit katha, bhajan aur seva ka aayojan hota hai.

📖

Katha Vachak — Preacher

Maharaj Ji Bhagwat Katha, Bhaktmal Katha, Ram Katha, Raas Leela aur Nikunj Ashtayam Katha ka niyamit aayojan karte hain — desh ke alag-alag sthanon par bhi jaate hain.

🤝

Seva Neta — Seva Leader

Radha Krishna Parivar Seva Trust (Govardhan, Mathura) ke madhyam se wo janseva, garib kalyan aur dharmik shiksha ka kaam karte hain.

📡

Digital Pracharak

Nimbark TV aur YouTube ke zariye unke pravachan live hote hain. 4 lakh se zyada subscribers aur 89 million views ke saath wo digital bhakti marg ke pracharak hain.

🧘

Sadhak — Spiritual Practitioner

Barsana ke Maan Mandir mein unhone teen saal gufa kakshe mein sadhana ki. Unka jeevan tyag, tapasya aur Radha-Krishna ki aradhana ka pratyaksha udaharan hai.

🌍

Braj Yatra Margdarshak

"Braj Chaurasi Kos Darshan" jaise yatrao ka Maharaj Ji niyamit aayojan karte hain, jismein shradhalu Braj ke pavitra sthano ka darshan karte hain.

✦   Aatmik Yatra — Spiritual Journey

Prarambh — Beginning

Sant-Sewa aur Satsang

Apne jeevan ke shuruaati dino se hi Maharaj Ji ne saints ka satsang shuru kiya. Unka antar-man Radha-Krishna ki bhakti ki aur khincha aur unhone sab kuch chhod Braj ki raah pakdi.

Teen Saal — Three Years

Vrindavan Mein Niwas

Pahle teen saal Maharaj Ji ne Vrindavan mein bitaye jahan unhone Swami Shri Krishnanand Ji Maharaj, Thakur Shri Ghanshyamdas Ji aur Shri Jagannath Baba jaise mahaan Santons ki seva aur satsang ka labh uthaya.

Barah Saal — Twelve Years

Barsana Mein Sadhana

Baad mein Maharaj Ji ne poore barah saal Shridham Barsana mein niwas kiya. Maan Mandir ke gufa kakshe mein teen saal ki gahan sadhana ki. Yahi par Bhagwan Shiv ne swapna mein darshan dekar Bhagwan Shri Krishna ke aishwarya roop (Shri Vishnu) ki sharanaagati karaai.

108 Kutiya

Ashram ki Sthapna

Barsana mein hi Maharaj Ji ne 108 Kutiya ashram ki neev rakhi — jo aaj ek prasiddh dharmik kendra hai jahan niyamit katha, bhajan, seva aur Braj-darshan ka aayojan hota hai.

Seva Trust

Radha Krishna Parivar Seva Trust

Govardhan, Mathura mein unhone Radha Krishna Parivar Seva Trust (Registered) ki sthapna ki jiske zariye samaj-seva, dharmik shiksha aur garib kalyan ke kaam niyamit roop se kiye jaate hain.
