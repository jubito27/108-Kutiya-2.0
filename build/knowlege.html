Braj Vrindavan Madhuri

ब्रज वृन्दावन माधुरी

स्वामी करुण दास जी महाराज द्वारा रचित यह अत्यंत दिव्य ग्रंथ ब्रज भूमि की असीम मधुरता का वर्णन करता है। इसमें श्री राधा-कृष्ण की नित्य लीलाओं, वृन्दावन के पवित्र कुंजों, और निष्काम प्रेम-भक्ति के मार्ग को बड़े ही सजीव व भावपूर्ण ढंग से प्रस्तुत किया गया है। यह पवित्र ग्रंथ महाराज जी के दैनिक 'सत्संग कृपा प्रसाद' प्रवचनों का मुख्य आधार भी है।

A profound and highly revered manuscript detailing the divine sweetness (Madhuri) of the Braj region. It masterfully explores the intimate, eternal pastimes of Shri Radha-Krishna, the esoteric significance of Vrindavan's sacred groves, and the ultimate path of unconditional devotion. This literature is prominently featured and deeply analyzed in the daily Satsang Kripa Prasad discourses delivered by Swami Karun Dass Ji.

Prem Pataka Shodashi

प्रेम पताका षोडशी

"प्रेम पताका षोडशी" भक्ति मार्ग का एक अनमोल रत्न है। 'षोडशी' का अर्थ है सोलह, जो इस ग्रंथ में वर्णित शुद्ध प्रेम और परा-भक्ति की सोलह विभिन्न अवस्थाओं और दिव्य गुणों को दर्शाता है। यह उत्कृष्ट रचना साधक का मार्गदर्शन करती है कि कैसे हृदय में भगवान के प्रति सर्वोच्च और निष्काम प्रेम की ध्वजा (पताका) को फहराया जाए।

An absolute masterpiece focusing on the ultimate philosophy of divine, selfless love. The term 'Shodashi' represents the sixteen magnificent virtues and progressive stages of pure Bhakti. By unraveling these stages, the book guides the true seeker on how to unfurl the 'Flag of Divine Love' (Prem Pataka) within their own heart, leading to absolute surrender at the lotus feet of the Divine.

"To read these scriptures is not merely an intellectual exercise; it is an intimate conversation with the divine essence of Braj, awakening the dormant seed of unconditional love within the soul."

— The Core Philosophy of 108 कुटिया

✦   Live Discourses & Traditions

Satsang Kripa Prasad

Swami Karun Dass Ji regularly conducts the highly anticipated Satsang Kripa Prasad sessions, where he provides deep, serialized commentary on texts like Braj Vrindavan Madhuri. These live broadcasts connect thousands of devotees directly to the spiritual vibrations of the ashram.

Shri Bhaktmal Katha

A cornerstone of the ashram's spiritual calendar is the grand Shri Bhaktmal Katha. During these multi-day discourses, Maharaj Ji narrates the extraordinary lives, sacrifices, and realizations of legendary saints, inspiring modern seekers to cultivate unwavering faith.

Ashtayam Seva

Central to the teachings found within his books is the practice of Ashtayam Seva—the continuous, eight-fold daily worship of Shri Radha-Krishna. It is a deeply immersive spiritual discipline designed to keep the devotee's consciousness anchored in divine remembrance throughout every phase of the day.
